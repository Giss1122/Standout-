    My Work

    Artwork/Designs

  • Design 1: "Separation"

    I made this design in my Design 1 class and ultimately loved the design and I would like to experiment with hoodie printing with this specific design.

  • Artwork 1: "The Dress"

    I made this Artwork in my Design 1 class and I really enjoyed and had fun using mixed media to create this piece. I made it using magazine cutouts, newspaper cutouts, markers, and colored pencils.

  • Artwork 2: "memory"

    • I made this when I was having a hard time and all I could think of was wanting to be in the comfort of my room. There is something so comforting about being in your own space. It is peaceful and that is what I wanted to show in this piece.

  • Artwork 3: "Fashion Design Collage"

    • This artwork was also made in my Design class and I love how assymetrical it is. I also used magazine cutouts to enhance the piece. It is all about fashion Design.

  • Artwork 4: "Magazine Girls"

    • There is no doubt that I get inspired using magazine cutouts to make collages. I love working with what I got and making it into something better and more fulfilling to me. I think using what I got is a reflection of my childhood and how I would use nothing to create my world into something.

  • Artwork 5: "Rage"

    • I chose this name for the artwork because I wanted to visually show an enraged human. I feel like rage is such a temporary, intense feeling and it is something I wanted to capture here. I think rage usually stems from things and obstacles that just happen out of nowhere.
